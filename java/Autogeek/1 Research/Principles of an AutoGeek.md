Strict card controls:
1. The card content must fully describe and match the specific vehicle.
	- Repeat sales of a vehicle within a year of the initial sale will be automatically indicated on the card to warn potential buyers.
	- An automatic duplicate card search will be implemented.
	- The entire generation or model description will be considered inappropriate.
	- The seller/organization description and driver contact information are prohibited.
	- Vehicle data will be supplemented from the vehicle title; manual editing of data entered from there is prohibited.
2. Regardless of the seller type, their market activity will be monitored.
	- For individuals, high activity may result in the seller being flagged to warn potential buyers.

Up-to-date information about vehicles and their manufacturers:
1. Find trusted sources (preferably the manufacturers themselves).
2. Collaborate with auto repair shops to obtain up-to-date recommendations.
