A person planning to buy a car.

*When and where does he use the app:*
The app can be opened on both a phone and a laptop.
When riding the subway, before bed, or during moments of rest and reflection. In other words, when his hands and time are free.

*What annoys him about the current process?:*
The constant rise in prices and the lack of clarity about where to choose a car: imported or used. What are the pitfalls and what are the risks?

*What do he want to achieve with your product?:*
To understand are his goals reachable or not. To choose a specific car from a variety of options.

*Reason for using the product (from most to least likely):*
- Finding the best option within a price range;
- Comparing several predetermined options;
- Finding cheaper alternatives to an unavailable option;
- Determining the market price of a car;
- Getting to know cars and their mechanics;

*Jobs to be Done:*
- When I want to find out the price of a car, I want to look at prices from a trusted source so I know what's real and what's definitely a scam, without having to research it myself.
- When I want to buy a car but don't know which one, I want to use a dedicated app to select several I like and compare them, without having to write down everything and research everything myself.
- When I want to buy a car but don't have enough money, I want to get a list of alternatives from the app so I can understand where I'll save money and what I'll lose. - When I want to buy a car, I want to get all the information about it from the app so I can understand what it does well and what it doesn't.
- When I want to know what a car should be like, I want to get information about its characteristics so I know what it can and can't do.
