A basic window and at the same time representation of entities:
- As window it contains list of entities in short view.
- As representation it is feed of entities in the form of short view.

Clicking on an entity's short view takes you to its full view and provides more information about it.
You can't open two full views of an entity in the one window.

Entities can have a short view (SV) and a full view (FV).
*Full view* - a view of an entity in the form of a full-fledged window with information, occupying the entire workspace or chat of an application or website and demonstrating full information about the entity.
*Short view* - a view of an entity as a partial window of information that occupies part of the workspace or chat area of ​​an application or website and displays partial information about the entity.

The workspace can only display one full view at a time, but several short ones.
