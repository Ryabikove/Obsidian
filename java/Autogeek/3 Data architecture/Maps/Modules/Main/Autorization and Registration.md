A window for registering or logging into the users account.

Principles:
- Registration or authorization occurs on an external website.
- Authorization opens up more opportunities for the user.
- Unauthorized users have access to the product's basic functionality.

During registration, you must:
- Enter your login
- Enter your password twice
- Authorization method (email or phone number)
- Gender
- Date of birth
- City of residence

During authorization, you must specify:
- Authorization type: login/phone number/email
- Password/verification code (only for phone number or email)
