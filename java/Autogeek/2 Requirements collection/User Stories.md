As a car buyer, I want to:
1. Find a car in the app and get its current price range to understand my budget;
2. Choose several cars I like and compare them in a table to understand which one is best for me.
3. I want a list of similar cars in the form of a filter table so I can understand where I'll save and what I'll lose.
4. I want information about the car's advantages and disadvantages in the app in the form of an article so I can understand how to use it.
5. I want information about its specifications so I know what the car is and isn't.
